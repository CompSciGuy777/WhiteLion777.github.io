Bio

I left medical school and desided to serve my army contract. I am now a Battalion Medical Officer stationed out of Fort Hood, TX. I am thankful to have a good job in these times of ecomomic turmoil. I enjoy working with and on computers and want to learn and do more. I am hungry to learn all I can. My next project is going to be an html5 game (maybe a series of them?).
